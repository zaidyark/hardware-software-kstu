About KsTU Hardware & Software Unit

The Kumasi Technical University Hardware and Software Unit is dedicated to providing comprehensive technical support to all staff and students. Our team of certified technicians specializes in resolving a wide range of hardware and software issues.

We understand that technical problems can disrupt academic and administrative work, which is why we've developed this efficient support system to ensure quick resolution of all technical issues.

Our services cover computer hardware repairs, software installation and troubleshooting, network connectivity issues, printer problems, and much more.
